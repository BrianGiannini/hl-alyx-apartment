===========

Car hatchback a01 fully assembled. Functional side and rear doors.
Rear glass and windscreen that breaks when shot. Made in a tutorial
and handed out mostly as-is. If you need further changes right-click
> Selected prefabs > Collapse on prefab.

Link to tutorial: https://youtu.be/tBUWv_rsxD8

How to:
  1. Drag .vmap file to <SDK>/content/hlvr/maps/prefabs
  2. From withing hammer, click prefab tab, find the prefab and drag it onto the floor and position and rotate if necessary.
